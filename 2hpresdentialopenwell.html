2 HP Openwell resdential pumps

The 2 HP open well residential pump is a robust and powerful solution designed for efficient water supply in both residential and small agricultural applications. Built from corrosion-resistant materials, including a sturdy stainless steel body and copper rotor, this pump ensures long-lasting durability and reliable performance. Its submersible design allows it to be fully immersed in open wells, borewells, or surface water sources, making it highly versatile. With a 2 HP motor, it provides a high discharge rate while maintaining energy efficiency, reducing operational costs. The pump is easy to install and maintain, making it ideal for irrigation, garden watering, and domestic water supply needs. It also comes with a control panel for easy operation and monitoring, and a protective powder-coated finish for enhanced durability.
