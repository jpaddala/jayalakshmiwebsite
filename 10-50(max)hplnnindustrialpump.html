10-50HP Horizontal Centrifugal Pump

The LNN family of pumps is the outcome of many years experience in the design and manufacture of axially split casing pumps. These pumps are axially split, double suction pumps with design featuring 145 impeller and volute combinations resulting in quiet operation, low NPSH requirements, and high efficiency operation in all water applications. Typical applications include water cooling, high and low water lift, raw water transfer, cargo loading and unloading, heating, ventilation and air conditioning and fire fighting.
