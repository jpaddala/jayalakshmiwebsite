V6 Radialflow Submersible Pump

The V6 radial flow submersible pump is engineered for high-efficiency performance in applications like irrigation, water supply, and drainage, with a capacity of up to 1800 liters per minute and a head range of 30 to 200 meters. It is available in motor power options ranging from 3 HP to 15 HP and is compatible with standard voltages of 230V or 415V. Equipped with a radial flow impeller, this pump is optimized for high-pressure water delivery. Constructed from durable stainless steel and cast iron, the pump weighs between 60-90 kg and supports both vertical and horizontal installations. Its corrosion-resistant materials ensure longevity, while built-in thermal overload protection reduces downtime and maintenance. Suitable for both residential and commercial use, this pump excels in agricultural irrigation, groundwater extraction, and industrial dewatering, offering a reliable and safe solution for demanding water management tasks.
