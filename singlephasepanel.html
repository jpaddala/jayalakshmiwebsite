Single Phase Panel Board

A single-phase submersible panel board is a specialized electrical control unit designed for underwater applications. It houses essential components like circuit breakers, switches, and fuses to protect and control the operation of submersible pumps used in water wells, irrigation systems, and other underwater environments. These panels are typically constructed with corrosion-resistant materials and are equipped with features like watertight seals and grounding terminals to ensure safe and reliable performance in harsh underwater conditions.
