3 HP Solar Pump

The 3 HP solar pump is an eco-friendly, cost-effective solution for agricultural irrigation, powered entirely by solar energy and designed for sustainable farming. It offers a reliable water supply without reliance on electricity or fuel, making it ideal for areas with limited power infrastructure. With a capacity to pump up to 500 liters per minute, this solar pump ensures efficient irrigation for small to medium-sized farms. Constructed from high-quality, corrosion-resistant materials, it is durable, low-maintenance, and long-lasting. Featuring a high-performance motor and advanced solar panels, it maximizes energy efficiency even in low sunlight. Easy to install and operate, the 3 HP solar pump is perfect for drawing water from wells, ponds, or rivers, providing farmers with a sustainable, low-cost, and reliable water management solution.
