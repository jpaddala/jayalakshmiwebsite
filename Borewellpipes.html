HDPE Pipes

HDPE pipes are manufactured as per IS 4984:1995 standard, available in 20mm to 630mm sizes in different pressure classes. These pipes are used to carry potable water in domestic, rural & agricultural pipelines.

Available in PN2.5 to PN16 pressure class in PE63, PE80, and PE100 grade, these pipes are manufactured according to Indian standards.
