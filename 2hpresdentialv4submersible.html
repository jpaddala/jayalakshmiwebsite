2 HP V4 Submersible Pump

The 2 HP V4 submersible pump is a robust and efficient solution for demanding water needs, ideal for residential, agricultural, and light industrial applications. It is built with a stainless steel body for durability and features a mixed flow impeller, providing a high water discharge rate. The pump is equipped with a water-filled motor for efficient cooling and prolonged operation, even under low voltage conditions. With a head range of 40 to 110 meters and a maximum discharge rate of up to 120 liters per minute, it is suitable for borewells of 100 mm and above. This pump is easy to install and requires minimal maintenance, making it perfect for water supply systems, irrigation, and overhead tank water lifting.
