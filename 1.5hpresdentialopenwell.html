1.5 HP Openwell resdential pumps

The 1.5 HP open well residential pump is a high-performance solution for consistent and reliable water supply in residential and small agricultural applications. Constructed from corrosion-resistant materials, including a durable stainless steel body and copper rotor, this pump is built to withstand challenging environments. Its submersible design allows for full immersion in open wells or reservoirs, making it ideal for drawing water efficiently. With a powerful 1.5 HP motor, it delivers a higher discharge rate and is energy-efficient, reducing operational costs. The pump is easy to install, maintain, and includes a control panel for convenient operation. Suitable for irrigation, garden watering, and household water supply, it offers long-lasting performance, enhanced by a powder-coated finish for additional protection.
