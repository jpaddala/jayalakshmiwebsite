Centrifugal Monoblock Pump

The centrifugal monoblock pump is a reliable and efficient solution for agricultural irrigation and water supply, with a capacity of up to 3000 liters per minute and a head range of 8 to 90 meters. Available in motor power options from 1 HP to 10 HP, it supports both single-phase (230V) and three-phase (415V) power supplies. Constructed from durable cast iron with brass or stainless steel impellers, the pump is corrosion-resistant and lightweight for easy handling. It features thermal overload protection for safe operation and is designed for continuous use, minimizing maintenance while providing consistent water flow.
