Solar Pumps

Solar Water Pumps Leading manufacturers offer solar water pumps as a sustainable and cost-effective alternative to traditional fuel-powered systems. These solar pumps are designed for durability, reliability, and efficiency, helping reduce carbon emissions while offering zero running costs. With a range of 1 HP to 20 HP, solar pumping systems are ideal for agricultural and rural applications, ensuring consistent water supply and easy integration with irrigation systems. These pumps are proudly empaneled with the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE) for both On-Grid and Off-Grid projects.

Jayalakshmi Engineerings is a trusted name in this sector for over 50 years and offers a wide range of solutions, from pumps to wires and valves—everything needed for pumping solutions for residential purposes.
