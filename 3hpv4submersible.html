V4 Submersible Pump

The 3HP V4 submersible pump is a versatile, reliable solution for both agricultural and domestic use, delivering high water output and consistent performance even in deep borewells. With its multi-stage design, it ensures optimal water flow with low energy consumption, making it cost-effective for continuous use. Built with durable, corrosion-resistant materials, it guarantees long-lasting performance in various water conditions. Ideal for irrigation, it efficiently pumps groundwater for crop watering, while also providing a steady supply of water for gardening, cleaning, and tank filling in residential areas.

Its single-phase compatibility makes it perfect for households and farms, and the water-filled motor ensures better cooling and extended operational life. ISI-certified, this pump offers guaranteed quality, efficiency, and reliability for any water management needs.
